What is technical here?

Camera frames are converted into 21 hand landmarks by MediaPipe. This app normalizes the hand pose, records temporal trajectories, resamples them to a fixed length, classifies gestures with Dynamic Time Warping and kNN-style prototypes, handles uncertainty with confidence thresholds, accepts active-learning feedback, and uses a tiny contextual-bandit policy for autonomous pet behavior.
